About Us:

We are here to train you. We have taken training in International Gym for 5 years. We are here to help you to be fit enough. We lead, instruct, and motivate individuals or groups in exercise activities,including cardiovascular exercises (exercises for the heart and blood circulation), strength training, and stretching.We work with people of all ages and skill levels. A personal trainer is an individual certified to have a varying degree of knowledge of general fitness involved in exercise prescription and instruction. They motivate clients by setting goals and providing feedback and accountability to clients. ... Qualified personal trainers recognize their own areas of expertise They start by evaluating their clients' current fitness level, personal goals, and skills. Then, they develop personalized training programs for their clients to follow, and they monitor the clients' progress.
